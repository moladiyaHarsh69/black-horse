Kathmandu Durbar Square

This place is one of the three regal Durbans of Nepal. It has witnessed the rule of one king after the other for a very long time. New rulers were crowned here with the beats of drums and trumpets that filled the place. One of the best places to visit in Kathmandu, this is the one place where you can explore the true history of Nepal.

The Durbar, located in Kathmandu, is renowned as a World Heritage Site by UNESCO and is famous for its vivid showcases of craftsmen and artists. People with fine and eloquent taste should add this place, as one of the best things to do in Nepal on their bucket list. To this date, this place remains the most remarkable legacy of Kathmandu, even after earthquakes that took a toll on the building, it still retained its original glory.
